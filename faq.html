Simple Pricing Models

We offer a selection of pricing options, ranging from pay-as-you-go plans to high-volume plans for enterprises that require a lot more minutes per month.

  • Pay As You Go

    Pay-as-you-go plans are our entry-level plans, perfect for those looking for a virtual phone number who don't plan on needing an exceptional number of minutes each month. Pay-as-you-go plans allow you to pay for the minutes as you use them rather than pre-purchasing a set number of minutes every month.

  • Minutes-Included Plan

    For those looking for a certain amount of minutes each month, a minutes-included plan is a perfect option. These plans come in a variety of sizes, from starter packages to medium and large monthly minute packages. If you know the general estimate of minutes you need each month, simply select the plan size that fits your needs. And if you need more minutes than your plan allows for, additional minutes can be purchased on a per-minute basis at a price commensurate with your plan.

  • High-Volume Packages

    High Volume Packages are priced on a case-by-case basis. These plans are ideal for those who expect to use more minutes than what's included in our Large Packages. Please contact one of our dedicated sales managers to build a customized high-volume plan that suits your business's needs.

    Perks of a high-volume plan include:Volume Pricing
    Dedicated Account Manager
    Instant activation
    Dedicated support team

  • Annual vs. Monthly Billing

    All of our plans are available to be billed either monthly or annually for a reduced cost. Therefore, if you plan to use roughly the same amount of minutes every month, you can purchase an annual plan at a discounted price compared to a monthly plan of the same size.

    If you are unsure of how many minutes you will need each month, we recommend starting with a monthly plan. That way, you can scale your minutes as needed. Then once you have a better understanding of how many minutes you need every month, you can switch to an annual plan.

  • Cancellation

    You are not required to sign any long-term contract with us, so you can cancel your service at any time. If you have any questions about billing or wish to cancel your account, you can contact us, and we will assist you with canceling or closing your account.
